The Big Crunch

A research vault — three open questions.

This is my research vault. I have three areas of interest, and I want to write toward findings rather than just collect links.

  1. What's causing the "big crunch" in modern society? Where are the leverage points? How can we make life enjoyable and free again?
  2. What's going on with men, and how do we fix it?
  3. Why are people alive?

The last one is more of an interview angle, but I do want to research it and write about my findings. Select any sentence here to leave a comment — a highlight will anchor to the exact words, and the note travels with them even if the surrounding paragraph changes.
